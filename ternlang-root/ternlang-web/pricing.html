About the Business Source License (BSL 1.1)
The restricted crates (ternlang-ml, ternlang-moe, ternlang-hdl, ternlang-runtime, ternlang-mcp, ternlang-api) are source-visible but may not be used for commercial purposes without a license from RFI-IRFOS. On 2030-04-03, they automatically convert to Apache 2.0.

Non-commercial use (research, personal projects, evaluation) is permitted under all tiers. To embed BSL crates in a commercial product, contact licensing@ternlang.com.
